What is CMYK?


Join us on Saturday, November 4, for a day of learning and practicing Graphic Design through a series of talks and friendly competition.

Just starting in your design journey? No sweat. We're excited to bring you a series of keynotes and workshops, presented by designers from Google, Dropbox, Adobe, Palantir, Everlane and Gladly.

Work with team to create a Graphic Design campaign for a local business, and get feedback from designers before delivering an exciting 3-minute pitch for your designs!

The event will be hosted at Jacobs Hall at the Jacobs Institute for Design Innovation. Coffee, bagels and lunch will be provided.
